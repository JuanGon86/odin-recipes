Pizza

Description

Pizza ia a savory Italian dish that typically consists of a round, flattened dough thats topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. Once the pizza is topped with the desired ingredients, it is baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted. With many diffrent variety of toppings it can be customized to suit individual preferences. It is a popular and widely enjoyed food found around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven typically 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to the desired size.
  3. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over the entire pizza until the sauce is no longer seen.
  5. Add desired toppings.
  6. Bake pizza 15-20 mins until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from oven and let it cool for a couple minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
