Burrito

Description

A burrito i a tortilla filled with multiple ingredients. Typically it includes rice, beans, meat, cheese, and salsa. Sometimes sour cream or guacomole are added for a little razzle dazzle. Everything is wrapped in the tortilla making it very portable to enjoy on the go. Burritos can be customized to suit individual tastes and dietary preferences.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Large Tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Rice and beans
  • Any other ingredients you want to add

Steps

  1. Cook whichever meat you prefer to your liking
  2. Prepare the rice and beans
  3. Lay down the large tortillas
  4. Put all ingredients on top of tortilla and wrap tightly
  5. Include some jalepenos for some spice
